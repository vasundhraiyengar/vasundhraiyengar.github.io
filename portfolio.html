About the project

Duration: 10/09/18-12/11/2018

The aim of this project was to work with ThriveTracker, a Sarasota based online campaign management company to design a responsive mobile web application for their existing web portal. ThriveTracker is the ultimate mobile and web campaign management solution for affiliates. With efficient campaign management tools at clients’ fingertips, they can focus on competition, strategy, and scale of their business. The design problem of this project centers around providing on-the-go campaign management features for ThriveTracker’s clients within the constraints of a smaller screen size and limited functionality while not compromising on the quality of user experience.
